Switchboard

Switchboard is a software alternative to a Private Branch Exchange (PBX) phone network. The large, upfront investment costs required for a hardware PBX setup are a burden to businesses with less than 100 employees and are generally impractical for businesses with less than 10 employees. We can now  host telephone service remotely, replicate PBX dialplans and provide  standard enterprise telephony features to small businesses.
